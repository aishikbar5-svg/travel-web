About Neo Travel

Your trusted partner in creating unforgettable travel experiences since 2009

Our Story

At Neo Travel, we believe that travel is more than just visiting new places—it's about transforming your perspective, creating lasting memories, and discovering the beauty of our world.

Founded in 2009, Neo Travel began with a simple mission: to make extraordinary travel experiences accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of passionate travelers has grown into a trusted name in the travel industry, serving over 10,000 happy travelers from around the globe.

Our team of dedicated travel experts shares a common passion for exploration and a commitment to exceptional service. We don't just plan trips—we craft experiences that become cherished memories.

Our Mission:

"To inspire and enable people to explore the world, creating personalized journeys that transform lives and create lasting connections."

Our Vision:

"To be the most trusted and innovative travel company, known for creating extraordinary experiences that exceed expectations."
